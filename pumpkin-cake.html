pumpkin-cake

Description

This delicious pumpkin cake is baked in a sheet pan. It's lightly spiced, very moist, and great for potlucks. When cool, top with your favorite cream cheese frosting. Very tasty cake.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • oil
  • pumpkin
  • eggs
  • salt

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl and beat the wet ingredients in another
  2. Beat the flour mixture into the wet mixture
  4. Stir in the nuts (if using) and pour it into a prepared sheet pan
  5. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean
